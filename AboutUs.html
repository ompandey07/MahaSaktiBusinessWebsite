About Mahashakti Lubricants

Premier Trading & Packaging Solutions Since Decades

For over two decades, Mahashakti Lubricants Private Limited has stood as a beacon of excellence in the lubricants and automotive industry. We are more than just suppliers - we are partners who understand the heartbeat of every engine and the precision required for every mechanical operation.

From our headquarters in the heart of Chitwan to serving industries across Nepal, we have built our reputation on unwavering quality, trusted relationships, and comprehensive solutions that keep Nepal's industries moving forward with confidence and reliability.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the lubricants industry in Nepal, Mahashakti Lubricants Private Limited has grown from a small startup to a leading provider of comprehensive trading and packaging solutions.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to deliver premium quality lubricants, grease items, and furnace oils that meet international standards while serving the unique needs of Nepalese industries.

Today, we stand proud as a trusted name in the industry, with our expertise spanning from import and supply operations from markets like Alkatra to comprehensive local distribution networks.
