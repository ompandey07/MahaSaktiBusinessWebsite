About Mahalami Lubricants

Our Story

Founded over two decades ago, Mahalami Lubricants Pvt Ltd began with a vision to provide high-quality lubricant solutions to industries across Nepal. Our journey started with a commitment to excellence, partnering with leading international brands to deliver products that meet the highest standards of performance and reliability.

Today, we are proud to be a trusted name in the lubricants industry, offering a comprehensive range of products that cater to both industrial and automotive needs. Our dedication to innovation and customer satisfaction drives us to continuously improve and expand our offerings.

Our Mission

At Mahalami Lubricants, our mission is to empower industries and individuals with premium lubricant solutions that enhance efficiency, reduce wear, and promote sustainability. We strive to deliver unparalleled quality and service, fostering long-term partnerships with our clients across Nepal.
