About Mahashakti Lubricants

Mahashakti Lubricants Private Limited stands as a premier name in the lubricants and automotive industry, offering comprehensive trading and packaging solutions. We specialize in a diverse range of products including lubricants, automotive products, grease items, furnace oils, and complete automotive services including spare parts for heavy and small vehicles.

Our company operates with multiple specialized divisions: our core trading operations that handle procurement and distribution of premium lubricants, our specialized packaging services, and our comprehensive automotive services division that provides spare parts, maintenance, and repair services for both heavy machinery and light vehicles.

Our expertise extends from import and supply operations of international lubricants and Bitumen products to complete automotive solutions including genuine spare parts, vehicle maintenance, and repair services, ensuring comprehensive support for all your industrial and automotive needs.

Our Mission & Vision

At Mahashakti Lubricants, our mission is to be Nepal's most trusted partner in both lubricant solutions and automotive services. We believe that every vehicle deserves the best care, whether it's through our premium lubricants that keep engines running smoothly or our comprehensive automotive services that ensure vehicles stay on the road safely and efficiently.

We envision a future where Nepalese industries and vehicle owners have access to world-class lubricants and automotive solutions right at their doorstep. Our commitment goes beyond just selling products - we build lasting relationships by providing complete automotive care, from the smallest spare part to major vehicle maintenance, ensuring our customers' success drives our own growth.

Through continuous innovation in our trading practices, packaging excellence, and automotive service delivery, we strive to set new standards in the industry while maintaining the personal touch that has defined our journey for over two decades. Every lubricant we trade and every vehicle we service reflects our dedication to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.
