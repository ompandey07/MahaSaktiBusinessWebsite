About Mahashakti Lubricants

Mahashakti Lubricants Private Limited is a premier name in the lubricants industry, specializing in both trading and packaging solutions. We provide comprehensive services for lubricants, grease items, and furnace oils across Nepal and beyond.

Our expertise in import and supply operations from international markets, including Alkatra, ensures that we deliver only the finest quality products to meet diverse industrial needs.

Our Mission & Vision

Mahashakti Lubricants started with the vision of becoming Nepal's leading provider of premium lubricant solutions. Through our comprehensive trading and packaging services, we serve as a complete solution provider for industries across the region.

From high-grade engine oils to specialized hydraulic fluids and furnace oils, we ensure that every product meets international standards while being accessible to local markets.
